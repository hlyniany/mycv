# Moves that recurred within this batch

- **Tool-stack titles:** Many titles lead with or heavily include tools such as `n8n`, `Make`, `Zapier`, `HubSpot`, `ActiveCampaign`, `Klaviyo`, `Airtable`, `GoHighLevel`, `ManyChat`, or `Zoho`.
- **“Not just connect tools” positioning:** Several profiles distinguish architecture/systems from simple tool connection, e.g. `“I don't just connect tools”`, `“not just ‘connect tools’”`, `“I design systems, not isolated automations.”`
- **Manual-work pain hooks:** Repeated pains include copy-pasting leads, manual CRM sync, manual DM handling, reporting, admin work, and routine business processes.
- **Revenue framing:** Several profiles describe automation/email/CRM as revenue infrastructure: `“leaking revenue”`, `“stable revenue channel”`, `“revenue-generating ecosystems”`, `“high-performance revenue engines”`.
- **Sectioned checklist format:** Many overviews use emoji headings and checklist sections like `WHAT I DO`, `How I can help`, `Why Work With Me`, `What you’ll achieve`, `Tools I Work With`.
- **Quantified savings claims:** Multiple profiles include time or efficiency claims such as `60–80%`, `70–90%`, `10–20+ hours saved`, `40+ hours per employee monthly`, `90 minutes manual → 5 minutes fully automated`.
- **CTA forms:** Common CTAs include `Message me`, `Let’s talk`, `Let’s schedule a call`, `Hit the Invite button`, and diagnostic prompts like `Tell me where it hurts`.
- **Anti-chatbot / embedded AI framing:** Some AI profiles specify that AI should live inside workflows rather than exist as a standalone chatbot or demo.
